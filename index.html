Guiding Light

Guiding Light is the reason this website exists. It's a Discord bot that manages JTC (Join-To-Create) voice channels.
To make it work, set up a notification and JTC channels. When someone joins a JTC, a temporary channel is created.
It will also notify your members when this happens and will tell when it's deleted.
The bot is written in C++, using the library.
